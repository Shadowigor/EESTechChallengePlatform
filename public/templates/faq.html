FAQ

Who can be the participant of the competition?

The participants of the competition are required to:

  1. have between 18 and 26 years;
  2. currently attending studies relevant to EECS (Electrical Engineering and Computer Science);
  3. reside in the same country as the one the Local Round they are applying for is held.
What happens after the Local Round?

The winning team needs to confirm to the local organisers within 10 days after the Local Round is finished whether they will attend the Final Round. If no confirmation takes place or the team cancels, then the 2nd winning team will be promoted to the Final Round. However, the winning team can maintain all the awards from the Local Round, with the exception of travel reimbursement (if applicable) that will be awarded to the team that will proceed to the Final Round.

Participants’ cancellation

If the cancellations takes place, more than 10 days before the inauguration of Final Round, then the process of the previous question is followed.

The winning teams that are proceeding to the Final Round should be consisted of three members each.

If one member of a local winning team cannot attend the Final Round, then the team is allowed to proceed to the Final Round, but may not introduce any new/substitute member for the one that has cancelled. The remaining members of the team need to confirm within a week whether they will proceed or not. If no confirmation takes place within a week, then the whole team will be disqualified.

If more than one contestant is not attending the Final Round, that exact team will not proceed, but the second-winning one will.

In case there is a cancellation, please, note that the criteria for all teams will remain the same during the Final Round.

Who will cover my expenses for the Final Round if I win locally (food, travel, accommodation…)?

During the Final Round, the accommodation and food (3 meals per day) will be covered by the Organising Committee. Travel expenses will not be covered by the organization of the Final Round. Local Round Organizing Committees might be providing this, however, it depends on the local organization. You can contact the Local Round Organizing Committee for more information.

Can I join as an individual or only as a team?

It depends on the Local Round organisation, you should contact local organisers in order to get the information.

Who should I contact if I have some questions?

To contact the international team, you can send an email to eestech-challenge@eestec.net.

Each Local Round has the Main Organiser of the local event, and you can find their contacts on this website when you click on the current city.

For any question regarding the Local Round itself, we are kindly asking you to contact the local organisers since the process of responding will be way more efficient in that case.
