EESTEC Competition for Android

A contest of ideas and mobile applications!

4th Competition for Android, organised for the third consecutive year, is an EESTEC International project which is open to students from all over the world. This academical event aims at opening a door for creative innovation by deploying knowledge about programming and design.

In the past years, the usage of applications has reached immense popularity and their usefulness slowly transformed what was merely a luxurious advantage into a basic tool for daily needs in terms of information, communication, mobility, etc.

Because Android has became part of our everyday life, we ought to participate enhancing the quality of the services that such platform is providing. As young students and free thinkers, we can bring ourselves to understand the necessities of our generation and help in unknotting them.

4th EESTEC Competition for Android can be a great experience because not only it teaches you about useful technical tools, but it also pushes you to research and analyse in order to meet a demand.

This event will be an amazing opportunity for the students of your university to gain experience about programming and design, show their creativity, and build an application that could be used by millions of people from around the globe.

About EESTEC

Electrical Engineering STudents' European assoCiation

EESTEC is a non-political, non-profit organization of and for Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) students at universities, institutes and technical schools in Europe awarding an engineering degree. The aim is to promote and develop international contacts and the exchange of ideas among the students of EECS. The association achieves its aim through improving technical knowledge of EECS students, introducing them to the industry and the educational system of other countries. The association was founded in The Netherlands in 1986. Since then, EESTEC has spreaded all around Europe and is now connecting over 4000 students from 32 countries and 55 universities. EESTEC addresses students with skills, openness, enthusiasm and ambition. It connects them to a wider range of opportunities in the educational as well as the professional world.

FAQ & Rules

What is EESTEC Competition for Android?

EESTEC Competition for Android is a contest of ideas and mobile applications, developed for the Android platform.
The project is organized for the 4th consecutive year by Electrical Engineering STudents European assoCiation.

Who can participate?

This Contest is open to anyone under 30 years old, coming from all around the world.

Prizes coming soon...

Best Design

Rewards the most skilled team with the best implemented idea from the design perspective. The award will go to the app with the best (friendliest) user interface.

Best Innovation

Rewards the most innovative idea. This award’s purpose is to encourage participants who didn’t have enough skills to complete the implementing part, but they came up with a great idea and work towards developing it to an application.

People's choice

A new prize category that has been added this year, awards the most popular app. The app can be promoted by the team in social media and be also uploaded in Google Play before the second deadline. The most liked one or the one with the best rating on Google Play will have a non monetary prize (special certificate, etc).

Best overall score

Rewards the team with the highest total score in all judging criteria. So, the team which gathers the above characteristics, skillfully designed , innovative, popular and stands out will have this award.
